# Postdoctoral Position in Algebra in Brussels 

The Department of Mathematics at Vrije Universiteit Brussel invites
applications for a postdoctoral position in algebra, within the framework of
the FWO-DFG Weave Project with Germany (grant G043326N). The position can
start as soon as possible. The initial appointment is for two years, with the
possibility of renewal for up to one additional year.

The gross monthly salary follows the standard postdoctoral salary scale in
Belgium and depends on the candidate’s seniority and family status.

### Research area

Hopf algebras, in particular Nichols algebras and deformation theory. Expertise
in these areas, or in modular representation theory, or permutation group
theory is required.

### Requirements

Applicants must hold a PhD in mathematics before the starting date, and no
later than September. A good command of English is required. The VUB wants to
be a reflection of the society where everyone's talent is valued, regardless of
gender, age, religion, skin color, migration background, disability and
neurodiversity.

### Duties

Applicants are expected to undertake the following duties: The successful
candidate will conduct research related to Nichols algebras and deformation
theory, and collaborate with partners in Germany. The project includes regular
interactions with Heckenberger's research group in Marburg.

* Conduct research related to Nichols algebras and deformation theory.
* Collaborate with project partners in Germany.
* Engage in regular scientific interactions with the research group of István
  Heckenberger at Philipps-Universität Marburg.
* Report regularly on research progress within the local research group and
  during meetings with the collaborators in Marburg.
* Present research results at international conferences and workshops.
* Participate in the organization of a workshop in this research area, jointly
  held between Brussels and Marburg.
